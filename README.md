✅ Action Items (Overview Dashboard)


Active Customer Rate is low (63%) → Launch re-engagement email/SMS campaigns for the remaining 37% inactive customers
Electronics generates the highest revenue (3.5M) → Increase stock and marketing budget for this category
Grocery and Beauty have the lowest revenue → Try discounts or bundle offers to boost sales in these categories
Senior age group has the lowest revenue (0.2M) → Design targeted campaigns for this segment
Revenue spiked sharply in Q4 (2.41M) → Analyze the reason behind this growth and apply the same strategy throughout the year
Gender ratio is nearly equal (48%-48%) → Marketing campaigns can stay gender-neutral, but the "Other" segment (4%) needs separate research

✅ Action Items (Campaign Analysis Dashboard)


Mobile and Desktop are the top revenue sources (8.4M + 8.2M) → Further optimize the mobile and desktop UX of the website/app
Tablet and Unknown devices generate lower revenue → Have the analytics team check whether Unknown device tracking is working correctly
Organic Traffic drives the highest revenue (8.3M) → Invest more in SEO and content marketing
Direct Traffic is the lowest (6.8M) → Run brand awareness campaigns to increase direct visits
Repeat Purchase Rate is low (0.43) → Launch a loyalty program or reward system to increase repeat customers
Discount Impact is significant (361.12K) → Review the discount strategy to assess its real effect on profit margins
